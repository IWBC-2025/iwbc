Call for Papers

\

The International Web3 & Blockchain Conference (IWBC 2025) invites original research contributions in Web3, Blockchain, Business, and Computing. The conference aims to provide a platform for academics, industry professionals, and entrepreneurs to exchange ideas, showcase innovations, and discuss the latest advancements in blockchain technology.

\

Conference Tracks

\
    \
  • Blockchain & Web3 Technology: Scalability, security, privacy, decentralized applications (DApps), and interoperability.
    • \
  • Business, Finance & Tokenomics: DeFi models, smart contracts, fintech innovations, and regulatory challenges.
    • \
  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Science: Machine learning in blockchain, predictive analytics, and data-driven insights.
    • \
  • E-commerce & Marketing: NFT marketplaces, immersive brand experiences, and next-gen consumer engagement.
    • \
  • Computer Science & Systems: Cloud computing, distributed systems, edge computing, and cybersecurity.
    • \
\

Submission Guidelines

\

All submissions must be original and not under review elsewhere. The following categories are welcome:

\
    \
  • Full Papers: Detailed research contributions (4-8 pages).
    • \
  • Short Papers: Concise studies or case analyses (2 pages).
    • \
  • Posters: Abstract-based early-stage research and innovative ideas.
    • \
\

Submissions should follow the ACM LaTeX template. Please refer to the official template at: \ACM Proceedings Template.
