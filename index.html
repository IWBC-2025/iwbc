Welcome to IWBC 2025

The International Web3 & Blockchain Conference (IWBC 2025) brings together researchers, developers, and industry professionals to discuss cutting-edge advancements in decentralized applications, smart contracts, and blockchain security.

Conference Highlights:

  • Keynote speeches from top Web3 & blockchain experts
  • Panel discussions on DeFi, DAOs, and emerging trends
  • Networking opportunities with blockchain professionals
  • Workshops on smart contract development
