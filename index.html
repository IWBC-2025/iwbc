Welcome to IWBC 2025

The International Web3 & Blockchain Conference (IWBC 2025) aims to unite researchers, developers, entrepreneurs, and industry experts from across the globe to explore the dynamic convergence of Web3, Blockchain, Business, and Computing.

Conference Highlights:

  • Inspirational Keynotes by top Web3 & blockchain leaders
  • Panel discussions on DeFi, DAO ecosystems, and next-gen financial models
  • Networking mixers connecting startups, investors, and academic researchers
  • Hands-on workshops for smart contract development and blockchain integration

Key Conference Tracks

In order to foster interdisciplinary collaboration, IWBC 2025 offers multiple parallel tracks:

  • Track A: Blockchain & Web3 Technology
    Protocols, scalability solutions, decentralized applications, security, privacy
  • Track B: Business, Finance & Tokenomics
    DeFi models, digital assets, fintech innovations, regulatory challenges
  • Track C: Artificial Intelligence & Data Science
    Machine learning in blockchain, data-driven business insights, predictive analytics
  • Track D: E-commerce & Marketing
    Next-gen consumer engagement, NFT marketplaces, immersive brand experiences
  • Track E: Computer Science & Systems
    Cloud computing, distributed systems, edge computing, cybersecurity

Organizers & Sponsors

IWBC 2025 is organized by leading academic and industry partners in the blockchain and technology sector, with generous sponsorship from global tech innovators.

Why Attend?

  • Cross-disciplinary Collaboration – Meet experts in both business and computing fields.
  • Startup & Investor Matchmaking – Explore funding opportunities and strategic partnerships.
  • Hands-on Experiences – Join live demos, hackathons, and training sessions.
  • Cultural Experience – Enjoy the vibrant city of Guangzhou, known for its innovation & global commerce.
